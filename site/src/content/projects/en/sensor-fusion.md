A real-time sensor-fusion and obstacle-visualization system that combines ultrasonic radar with computer vision to detect
pedestrians and vehicles around the car.

Object detection runs on YOLOv3, with OpenCV and OpenGL ES handling image processing and rendering. Detections are presented
in a radar-style polar view that precisely marks obstacle coordinates and distance (5 m / 10 m rings), giving the driver an
intuitive surround-awareness interface.
